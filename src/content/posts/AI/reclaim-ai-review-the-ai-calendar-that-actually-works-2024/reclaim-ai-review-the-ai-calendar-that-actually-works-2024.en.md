## What Is Reclaim AI?

Reclaim AI is an **AI-powered calendar assistant** designed to eliminate the guesswork in scheduling. Instead of manually dragging tasks around your calendar or hoping for "open slots," Reclaim AI:

- **Scans your calendar** (including meetings, habits, and tasks) to find the best available time.
- **Automatically schedules** tasks, habits, and focus blocks without manual input.
- **Protects your focus time** by blocking distractions during deep work.
- **Syncs with your workflow tools** (Asana, Todoist, Jira, etc.) to keep everything in sync.

### How It Differs from Traditional Tools
Most calendar apps (like Google Calendar or Outlook) are **reactive**—they show you what’s already scheduled but don’t help you *plan* proactively. Reclaim AI is **proactive**:
- It **learns your habits** (e.g., "I always have a 3 PM meeting on Tuesdays").
- It **adapts to changes** (e.g., if a meeting moves, it reschedules your tasks).
- It **balances priorities** (e.g., "This task is urgent, but I need focus time first").

Think of it as a **personal scheduling assistant** that works in the background, not just a digital planner.
