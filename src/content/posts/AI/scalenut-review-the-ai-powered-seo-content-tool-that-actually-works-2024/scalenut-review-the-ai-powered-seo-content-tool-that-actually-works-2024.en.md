## Key Features: How Scalenut Works

### 1. **AI-Powered Content Writing with SEO Optimization**
Scalenut doesn’t just generate text—it **optimizes for search intent**. When you input a keyword, it:
- Analyzes top-ranking pages.
- Suggests **ideal content structure** (headings, word count, readability).
- Generates **AI-written sections** that align with search intent.

💡 *Example:* If you’re writing about "best project management tools," Scalenut will suggest subtopics like "price comparisons" or "best for remote teams" based on what Google ranks.

### 2. **SERP-Based Content Briefs**
Instead of guessing what Google wants, Scalenut **scrapes top-ranking pages** and gives you a **customized brief** with:
- **Word count** (based on competitors).
- **Keyword density** (how often to use target terms).
- **Readability scores** (Flesch-Kincaid, etc.).
- **Content gaps** (what competitors are missing).

This is **far more actionable** than generic SEO tools.

### 3. **Keyword Research & Clustering**
Scalenut’s **Keyword Research** tool helps you:
- Find **low-competition, high-intent keywords**.
- Group them into **content clusters** (e.g., "SEO tools" → "Surfer SEO vs. Clearscope").
- Discover **long-tail opportunities** that competitors miss.

### 4. **Real-Time SEO Scoring**
Before publishing, Scalenut **grades your content** on:
- **Keyword optimization** (are you using the right terms?).
- **Readability** (is it easy to scan?).
- **Content depth** (are you covering all key points?).

This helps you **fix weak spots before hitting publish**.
