## FAQ: Common Questions About Mixo

### **1. Is Mixo really free?**
Yes! The **Free plan** lets you create a basic landing page with a Mixo subdomain (e.g., `youridea.mixo.site`). Upgrade for custom domains and more features.

### **2. Can I use Mixo for e-commerce?**
No—Mixo is **not an e-commerce platform**. It’s designed for **landing pages, idea validation, and lead collection**. For selling products, use **Shopify, Gumroad, or Payhip**.

### **3. How customizable is Mixo?**
You can **change colors, fonts, and layout sections**, but it’s **not as flexible as Webflow or Squarespace**. Best for **simple, professional landing pages**.

### **4. Does Mixo work with my own domain?**
Yes! The **Pro plan ($19/month)** and up allow **custom domains**.

### **5. Can I export my Mixo site later?**
Currently, **no**. Mixo is a **hosted solution**, so you can’t export HTML/CSS. If you need full ownership, consider **Webflow or WordPress** instead.

### **6. How does Mixo’s AI generate content?**
You **describe your business idea**, and Mixo’s AI **generates copy, images, and structure** based on common best practices. You can **edit everything** afterward.

### **7. Is Mixo good for SEO?**
Mixo sites are **mobile-friendly and fast**, which helps SEO. However, **advanced SEO (like custom meta tags) requires the Pro plan**. For **serious SEO**, consider **WordPress + Yoast**.
