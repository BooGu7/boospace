## FAQ: Simplified Answered

### **1. Is Simplified really free?**
Yes, but the **free plan is limited**—no AI writing, basic templates, and no scheduling. The **Pro plan ($24/month)** unlocks all features.

### **2. Can I use Simplified for professional video editing?**
No. Simplified offers **basic video editing** (templates, trimming, captions) but **not advanced features** like CapCut or Premiere Pro.

### **3. Does Simplified work with Canva designs?**
Yes, but **only for imports**—you can’t edit Canva files directly in Simplified.

### **4. Is there a team plan?**
Yes, the **Team plan ($48/month for 3 users)** includes collaboration features.

### **5. Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes, Simplified offers **monthly billing with no long-term contracts**.

### **6. Does Simplified have a mobile app?**
Yes, but **some features (like advanced video editing) are limited** on mobile.

### **7. Is Simplified better than Canva + Buffer?**
**For some users, yes.** If you want **AI writing + scheduling + design in one tool**, Simplified can replace both. However, **Canva + Buffer is still better for advanced designers**.
