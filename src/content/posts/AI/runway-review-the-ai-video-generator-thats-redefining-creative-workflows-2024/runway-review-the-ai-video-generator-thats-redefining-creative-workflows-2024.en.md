## FAQ About Runway

### **1. Is Runway free to use?**
Runway offers a **free plan**, but **Gen-3 Alpha (the best model) requires a Pro subscription ($36/month)**. The free plan allows limited generations of older models.

### **2. Can Runway replace Adobe Premiere Pro?**
No—Runway is **not a full video editor**. It’s best for **AI-assisted generation and quick edits**, while Premiere Pro is for **professional post-production**.

### **3. How good is Gen-3 Alpha compared to other AI video tools?**
Gen-3 Alpha is **one of the best text-to-video models** available, with **better motion and realism** than most competitors (like Pika Labs or Synthesia).

### **4. Does Runway work offline?**
No—Runway is **browser-based** and requires an internet connection. There’s **no desktop app** (yet).

### **5. Can I use Runway for commercial projects?**
Yes, but you must **follow Runway’s content policies** (no misleading AI demonstrations, proper attribution if using AI-generated assets).

### **6. Does Runway have an API?**
Yes! The **Enterprise plan** includes API access, allowing developers to **automate video generation** (great for agencies and e-commerce).

### **7. How long does it take to generate a video in Runway?**
- **Short clips (5–10 sec):** ~30 seconds to 2 minutes.
- **Longer videos (30+ sec):** 5–15 minutes (depends on complexity).

### **8. Can I edit videos in Runway like in Premiere Pro?**
Runway has **basic editing tools** (trim, cut, add text), but it’s **not a full timeline editor**. For advanced editing, export to Premiere Pro or Final Cut.

### **9. Is Runway good for beginners?**
It has a **learning curve**, but the **free plan lets you experiment** before committing. Tutorials and community guides help.

### **10. What’s the biggest limitation of Runway?**
The **biggest downside** is that it’s **not a full video editor**—you’ll still need **Premiere Pro or After Effects** for professional post-production.
